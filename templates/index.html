What is Flight Club?

Flight Club is a web service that finds the cheapest flight to a destination within the next 6 months. As a user you will receive daily updates for flights found under your designated price, as well as have the option to instantly receive deals. All of our deals are sent via email in the dead of night so that you can wake up to a fresh set of deals each morning.
